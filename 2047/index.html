Empowering the Future with Technology

Viksit Bharat — a Developed India

Digital India 2047 envisions a technologically advanced and inclusive nation.
It builds upon the success of the Digital India initiative launched in 2015.
The vision aims to make India a global leader in innovation and digital governance.
Every citizen will be digitally empowered and connected to new opportunities.
It focuses on smart infrastructure, secure data systems, and digital literacy.
Entrepreneurship, research, and technology-driven growth are key pillars.
Together, these efforts will shape a Viksit Bharat — a Developed India by 2047.

Vision for Digital India 2047

By 2047, Digital India envisions a nation where every citizen is digitally empowered and connected, from the remotest village to the largest city. Seamless access to public services, education, and healthcare through digital platforms will ensure transparency and inclusivity. High-speed internet, AI, and advanced technologies will drive innovation, economic growth, and smart infrastructure. Digital tools will support farmers, students, and entrepreneurs alike, bridging the rural-urban divide. Strong data privacy and cybersecurity laws will protect citizens' rights in a trusted digital environment. India will lead globally in digital public infrastructure and technology-driven governance. Sustainability will guide the growth of digital ecosystems, with green data centers and smart cities. Digital India 2047 is a vision of inclusive progress, powered by technology and driven by people.
