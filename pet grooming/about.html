ABOUT US

At Furry Fresh, we put our love
for pets and their owners
into all that we do.

At Furry Fresh, we believe every pet deserves to look and feel their best.
We're a passionate team of certified groomers and lifelong animal lovers,
dedicated to providing gentle, high-quality grooming services in a safe
and stress-free environment.

Whether it’s a refreshing bath, a stylish haircut, or a nail trim, we tailor
every service to meet the unique needs of your furry friend. We use
premium, pet-safe products and modern grooming techniques to ensure
your pet leaves looking clean, feeling happy, and wagging with pride.

Our mission is simple: to treat your pets like family and make every
grooming visit a positive experience—for both of you.
Come see why pets (and their owners) love us!
